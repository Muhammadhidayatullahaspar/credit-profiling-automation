Column Descriptions:

  • Interest Rate: The interest rate applied to the loan
  • Grade: The credit risk category, from A (lowest) to G (highest)
  • Home ownership: The borrower's home ownership status, with several options
  • Purpose: The purpose of the loan application, with several options such as car, credit_card, debt_consolidation, and others
  • Verification Status: The verification status of the borrower's information, with options of Not Verified, Verified, and Source Verified
  • Months since loan issue date: The number of months since the loan was issued
  • Remaining outstanding principal amount: The amount of outstanding debt that has not been paid by the borrower
  • Total late fee received: The total amount of fees received for late payments
  • Recoveries: The amount of funds successfully recovered from borrowers who were late in paying
